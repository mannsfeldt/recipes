Haystacks

Classic no-bake chocolate oat cookies — rich cocoa, chewy oats, and shredded coconut set into little mounds on waxed paper. Ready in 20 minutes, no oven required.

  • Prep10 min
  • Cook5 min
  • Set30 min
  • Yield~30 cookies

Equipment

  • Medium saucepan
  • Waxed paper or parchment
Scale recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (400 g)white sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115 g)butter (cold/firm)
  • 4 tbsp (25 g)cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml)milk
  • 3 cups (270 g)rolled oats
  • 1 cup (80 g)shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp (4 g)vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. 1

    Line two baking sheets or a large counter area with waxed paper.

  2. 2

    Combine sugar, butter, cocoa, and milk in a medium saucepan. Bring to a full rolling boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.

  3. 3

    Boil hard for exactly 1 minute, continuing to stir. Remove from heat immediately.

  4. 4

    Working quickly, stir in rolled oats, coconut, and vanilla until fully combined.

  5. 5

    Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto the waxed paper. Let cool and set completely at room temperature, about 30 minutes.
