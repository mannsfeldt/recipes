Triple Threat Chocolate Cheesecake

Silky smooth cheesecake layered with semi-sweet and white chocolate filling, finished with a bittersweet chocolate ganache — the ultimate birthday showstopper.

  • Prep25 min
  • Bake50 min
  • Chill2 hr+
  • Servings8–10

Equipment

  • 8½-inch springform pan
  • Stand or hand mixer
Scale recipe

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 1/3 cups (150 g)chocolate wafer crumbs
  • 1/3 cup (75 g)butter, melted

Filling

  • 2 pkgs (500 g)cream cheese, softened
  • 1 can (300 mL) (300 ml)sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 (100 g)eggs
  • 4 oz (114 g)white chocolate, melted
  • 2 tbsp (30 g)hazelnut or almond liqueur (optional)
  • 4 oz (114 g)semi-sweet chocolate, melted

Ganache

  • 1/2 cup (125 ml)whipping cream
  • 4 oz (114 g)bittersweet chocolate

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 300 °F (150 °C). Combine chocolate wafer crumbs and melted butter. Press firmly onto the bottom of an 8½-inch springform pan.

  2. 2

    Beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in condensed milk and eggs until smooth. Pour half the batter into a second bowl.

  3. 3

    Stir white chocolate and liqueur into one bowl. Stir semi-sweet chocolate into the other.

  4. 4

    Pour the semi-sweet batter over the crust. Spoon the white chocolate batter on top. Bake 45–50 minutes until just set. Cool completely.

  5. 5

    Heat whipping cream until steaming. Add bittersweet chocolate and stir until melted and smooth. Spoon over the cooled cake; spread to cover top and sides.

  6. 6

    Garnish with chocolate curls if desired. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before slicing.

Notes: For clean slices, dip a sharp knife in hot water, wipe dry, then cut. Store covered in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
