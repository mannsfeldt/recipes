Bruschetta 'n Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breasts

Pounded chicken breasts rolled around a tomato-basil stuffing, baked in a roasted red pepper dressing and finished with bubbling mozzarella.

  • Prep15 min
  • Bake45 min
  • Total1 hr
  • Servings8

Equipment

  • Large freezer bag
  • Meat mallet or rolling pin
  • 13×9-inch baking dish
Scale recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can (19 fl oz) (540 ml)diced tomatoes with garlic and olive oil, undrained
  • 1 1/4 cups (140 g)shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup (10 g)chopped fresh basil
  • 1 pkg (120 g) (120 g)Stove Top stuffing mix for chicken
  • 8 small (900 g)boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/3 cup (80 ml)roasted red pepper Parmesan dressing

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350 °F. In a bowl, combine tomatoes (with liquid), ½ cup of the mozzarella, basil, and dry stuffing mix; stir until just moistened.

  2. 2

    Place 2 chicken breasts in a large freezer bag and pound to ¼-inch thickness. Repeat with remaining breasts, 2 at a time.

  3. 3

    Lay each pounded breast smooth-side down. Spread with ⅛ of the stuffing mixture, then roll tightly from the narrow end.

  4. 4

    Place seam-side down in a 13×9-inch baking dish. Pour dressing evenly over chicken. Bake 40 minutes.

  5. 5

    Sprinkle with remaining ¾ cup mozzarella. Bake 5 more minutes until cheese is melted and chicken reaches 170 °F.

Notes: For a Tex-Mex version, use diced tomatoes with jalapeños, cilantro instead of basil, and Tex Mex shredded cheese.
