Chicken & Broccoli Casserole

A creamy, comforting baked casserole of tender broccoli and chicken in a seasoned soup sauce, topped with golden bread crumbs and melted mozzarella.

  • Prep20 min
  • Cook37 min
  • Total57 min
  • Servings4–6

Equipment

  • Large saucepan
  • 9×13-inch casserole dish
Scale recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (568 g)cream-of-chicken soup
  • 3/4 cup (165 g)mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp (2 g)freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp (2 g)ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp (1.5 g)garlic powder
  • 1 pinch (1 g)each ground ginger and dried oregano
  • 1 large bunch (600 g)broccoli, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 3 cups (450 g)cubed cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup (55 g)fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup (60 g)grated mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350 °F. In a large saucepan combine cream-of-chicken soup, mayonnaise, pepper, coriander, garlic powder, ginger, and oregano. Set over medium heat and whisk frequently until fairly smooth and warm, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.

  2. 2

    Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the broccoli and cook 2 minutes; drain well. Stir broccoli and chicken into the soup mixture.

  3. 3

    Lightly butter a 9×13-inch casserole dish. Pour the chicken mixture in. Sprinkle evenly with bread crumbs then mozzarella.

  4. 4

    Bake in the centre of the oven for 30 minutes, or until golden and bubbling. Serve hot.

Notes: Rotisserie chicken works perfectly here. Drain the broccoli thoroughly so the sauce stays creamy and doesn't water down.
