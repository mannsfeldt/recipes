Gramma's Microwave Cheese Sauce

A quick, foolproof stovetop-style white sauce made entirely in the microwave — smooth, cheesy, and ready in under 5 minutes.

  • Prep2 min
  • Cook5 min
  • Total7 min
  • Servings4

Equipment

  • Large microwave-safe bowl
  • Whisk
Scale recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp (28 g)butter
  • 2 tbsp (16 g)all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml)cold milk
  • 4 oz (115 g)cheddar cheese, chopped or shredded

Instructions

  1. 1

    Melt butter in a large microwave-safe bowl, about 30–45 seconds.

  2. 2

    Whisk flour into the melted butter until smooth and no dry flour remains.

  3. 3

    Gradually whisk in the cold milk until fully combined and no lumps remain. Cold milk helps prevent lumps.

  4. 4

    Microwave on high for about 4 minutes, stopping to whisk thoroughly every 60 seconds, until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. 5

    Stir in the cheddar cheese until fully melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Notes: Written by Gramma for Tom. Use cold milk (not warm) — it gives you more time to whisk out lumps before the sauce sets. A pinch of nutmeg or dry mustard adds a nice depth.
