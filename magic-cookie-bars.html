Magic Cookie Bars

The classic five-layer bar — buttery graham cracker base, sweetened condensed milk, chocolate chips, coconut, and nuts — pressed and baked until golden.

  • Prep10 min
  • Bake30 min
  • Total40 min
  • Yield24 bars

Equipment

  • 13×9-inch baking pan
Scale recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (125 g)butter
  • 1 1/2 cups (165 g)graham wafer crumbs
  • 1 can (300 mL) (300 ml)sweetened condensed milk (Eagle Brand)
  • 1 cup (175 g)semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/4 cups (100 g)flaked coconut
  • 1 cup (120 g)chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350 °F (180 °C), or 325 °F (160 °C) for a glass dish.

  2. 2

    Melt butter in the baking pan in the oven.

  3. 3

    Sprinkle graham crumbs over the melted butter; stir to coat and press into an even layer.

  4. 4

    Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over the crumb layer. Sprinkle with chocolate chips, then coconut, then nuts. Press down firmly.

  5. 5

    Bake 25–30 minutes until lightly browned. Cool completely before cutting. Store loosely covered at room temperature.

Notes: Cool completely before cutting — they will fall apart if cut warm. For extra-clean squares, refrigerate 30 minutes after cooling.
