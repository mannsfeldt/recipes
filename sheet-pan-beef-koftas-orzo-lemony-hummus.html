Sheet Pan Beef Koftas with Orzo and Lemony Hummus Sauce

Spiced beef koftas roasted on a sheet pan with zucchini, bell pepper, and onion — served over lemony orzo with a creamy hummus and garlic sauce.

  • Prep10 min
  • Cook25 min
  • Total35 min
  • Servings2

Equipment

  • Rimmed baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Medium pot
  • Box grater
  • Zester
  • Strainer
Scale recipe

Ingredients

Produce

  • 1 medium (120 g) red onion, half grated / half diced
  • 1 medium (200 g) zucchini, cut into ½-inch rounds
  • 1 (150 g) sweet bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 (100 g) lemon, zested and juiced
  • 4 cloves (20 g) garlic, minced or grated

Koftas

  • 250 g (250 g) lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup (25 g) panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp (5 g) garlic salt

Shawarma Spice Blend

  • 1 tsp (2.5 g) ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp (1 g) ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp (1 g) smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp (0.5 g) ground turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp (0.5 g) ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp (0.5 g) ground allspice
  • 1/8 tsp (0.3 g) ground cardamom
  • 1/8 tsp (0.3 g) cayenne pepper

Orzo

  • 1 tbsp (14 g) olive oil
  • 170 g (170 g) orzo pasta (about 1 cup)
  • 1 tbsp (9 g) vegetable stock powder
  • 2 tbsp (28 g) unsalted butter

Lemony Hummus Sauce

  • 4 tbsp (60 g) hummus
  • 2 tbsp (28 g) mayonnaise

Instructions

Prep

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). Bring a medium pot of salted water to a boil. Peel the red onion and grate half of it into a large bowl; cut the remaining half into ½-inch pieces. Slice the zucchini into ½-inch rounds. Cut the bell pepper into 1-inch pieces. Zest and juice the lemon, keeping them separate. Mince or grate the garlic. Combine all shawarma spice blend spices in a small bowl and stir together — you'll use half for the koftas and half for the vegetables.

Koftas

  1. 2

    To the bowl with the grated onion, add the ground beef, panko, half the garlic, half the garlic salt, and half the shawarma spice blend. Season with black pepper and mix until evenly combined. Roll the mixture into 6 oval logs.

Bake

  1. 3

    On a parchment-lined baking sheet, toss the zucchini, bell pepper, and diced onion with the remaining garlic salt, remaining shawarma spice blend, and olive oil. Spread in a single layer. Arrange the koftas on top of the vegetables. Bake 14–20 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the koftas are cooked through (internal temperature 165°F / 74°C).

Orzo

  1. 4

    Add the orzo to the boiling water and cook 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until just tender but still firm to the bite. Reserve 1 tbsp of pasta water, then strain and return to the pot off heat.

  2. 5

    Add the vegetable stock powder, 1 tsp of the lemon zest, 1 tbsp of the lemon juice, butter, and reserved pasta water to the orzo. Stir to combine.

Sauce & Serve

  1. 6

    In a small bowl, stir together the hummus, mayonnaise, remaining garlic, and remaining lemon juice until smooth.

  2. 7

    Divide the orzo between plates. Top with the roasted vegetables and koftas. Drizzle the lemony hummus sauce over top. Serve with any remaining lemon wedges.

Notes: The Shawarma Spice Blend totals 1 tbsp (3 tsp) and is split equally between the koftas and the vegetables. If you make this often, pre-mix a larger batch and store in a jar — it keeps well for months and is great on chicken or roasted cauliflower too. Cumin and coriander are the backbone; don't skip those. Cayenne is optional if cooking for kids.
