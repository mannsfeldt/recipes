Best Ever Meatloaf

A moist, cheesy meatloaf from Pearl Croom's kitchen, filled with shredded carrot and cheddar, finished with a sweet ketchup-mustard glaze.

  • Prep20 min
  • Bake1 hr 30 min
  • Rest10 min
  • Servings6

Equipment

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Casserole dish or loaf pan
Scale recipe

Ingredients

Meatloaf

  • 2 medium (100 g)eggs
  • 2/3 cup (160 ml)milk
  • 2 tsp (12 g)salt
  • 1/4 tsp (1 g)black pepper
  • 3 slices (90 g)fresh bread, broken into small pieces
  • 1 medium (150 g)onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup (60 g)shredded carrot
  • 1 cup (115 g)shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 lbs (680 g)lean ground beef

Glaze

  • 1/4 cup (60 ml)ketchup
  • 1/4 cup (55 g)brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp (15 g)mustard

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350 °F.

  2. 2

    Beat eggs with a fork in a large bowl. Add milk, salt, pepper, and bread; beat until the bread disintegrates.

  3. 3

    Add onion, carrot, cheddar, and ground beef; mix well. Shape into a loaf in a casserole dish.

  4. 4

    Stir together ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard; spread over the top of the loaf.

  5. 5

    Bake at 350 °F for 1½ hours. Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.

Notes: From the kitchen of Pearl Croom. The cheddar inside keeps the meatloaf moist and adds a lovely savoury depth.
